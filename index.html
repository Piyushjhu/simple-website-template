About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Johns Hopkins University with extensive experience in developing automated experimental facilities, researching high-velocity impact loadings, and exploring shock responses in advanced materials. With a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Rhode Island and a strong background in composite fabrication and computational mechanics, I aim to innovate and lead in the field of extreme material applications.
